ABOUT ME

I'm a passionate UI/UX designer and a Computer Science engineering student at Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering, Mysuru. With a strong foundation in technology and a deep interest in user experience design, I strive to create seamless, user-friendly digital experiences.

My approach to design is a blend of problem-solving, creativity, and functionality, ensuring that every interface I craft is both visually appealing and highly intuitive. I am continuously learning and refining my skills in wireframing, prototyping, user research, and interaction design, with hands-on experience using tools like Figma and Adobe XD.

With my background in computer science, I bring a technical perspective to UI/UX design, allowing me to collaborate effectively with developers and create designs that are not just beautiful but also practical and scalable.

I'm always eager to learn, grow, and contribute to meaningful projects that enhance user experiences. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
